If you did not rent and occupy a property as your principal residence (main home) on October 1, 2020, or you were not listed as a renter on the lease, you do not qualify for the ANCHOR Benefit. Renters whose units are under a P.I.L.O.T (Payment in Lieu of Taxes) agreement are eligible to file for the ANCHOR benefit. We review applications to ensure renters meet the eligibility requirements. If you provide inaccurate information when filing and we send you a larger benefit as a result, You must repay any benefit amount you are not eligible to receive. If you have any questions, call 1-888-238-1233, Monday through Friday, 8:30 a.m.-5:30 p.m. ET, except State holidays.
